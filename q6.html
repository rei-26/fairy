Queen Clarion appears again, gliding down through soft beams of twilight. Her wings shimmer like distant stars, and her voice is gentle but resolute.

“To truly discover your fairy path,” she says, “you must face the fear that lingers deepest in your heart.”

The air stills. Even the wind listens.

What do you fear most?

  • 👣 Letting others down — the thought of someone counting on you, and failing them, makes your wings tremble.
  • 🌟 Being ordinary or forgotten — you long to shine so brightly, the world remembers your name in song.
  • 💥 Breaking something important — the fragile things you care about, your creations, your friendships, what if you ruin them?
  • 🌀 Not understanding your own powers — the unknown within you feels like a storm you can’t yet name.
