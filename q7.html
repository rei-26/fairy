💫 A Dream Shared

As dusk settles, you drift toward a moonlit clearing. Fireflies weave glowing trails around you, and the hush of the world feels like a lullaby.

A voice, not quite of this world, whispers softly:

“Share your dream. Just one — the truest wish from your fairy heart.”

  • 💕 To protect those I love and keep them safe. (Earth, Animal)
  • 🌳 To explore beyond Pixie Hollow, into the great unknown. (Explorer, Air)
  • 💧 To help others heal and feel deeply. (Water, Nurturer)
  • 💎 To create something that lasts forever. (Tinker, Legacy)
